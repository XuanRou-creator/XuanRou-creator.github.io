The Problem

People fail to execute their financial plans

Even when they know they should manage money better, emotional decisions and disconnected tools get in the way.

🔗

Disconnected from Real Goals

Users have clear goals — study abroad, buy a house, travel — but don't know how much to save, when to invest, or how to manage risk.

📱

Existing Tools Only Inform

Banking apps, robo-advisors, and budgeting tools only give recommendations. Users still have to make decisions and execute manually.

😰

Decisions Are Emotional

Buying FX at peak exchange rates, panic-selling during crashes, spending money originally reserved for future goals.

The Solution

GoalPilot — Your AI Financial Butler

Three integrated engines that take you from setting a goal to automatically executing the financial strategy.

01

Goal Engine

You input a goal — study abroad, buy a home, save for a wedding. GoalPilot auto-calculates required capital, time horizon, risk level, and monthly targets.

02

Planning Engine

Dynamically generates monthly saving plans, investment allocation, FX strategies, and risk adjustments based on market conditions and your cash flow.

03

Execution Engine ⭐

Automatically executes ETF investments, currency exchanges, savings allocation, and emergency reserve management — no manual action needed.
